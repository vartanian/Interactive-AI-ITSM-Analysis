Beyond the Core: External Integrations

While powerful, native Atlassian Intelligence has architectural limits. Executing tasks in external systems requires looking towards Atlassian Rovo and the rich ecosystem of third-party apps on the Marketplace.

Architectural Boundaries

Broader Enterprise Toolchain
Atlassian Rovo & Marketplace

For tasks requiring external data access and actions in third-party systems.

  • Connects to SharePoint, GitHub, etc.
  • "Agentic" AI performs complex tasks
  • Requires separate licensing
Native Atlassian Intelligence (JSM)

Powerful for automating processes *within* the Atlassian ecosystem.

  • Cannot generate rules with webhooks
  • Limited to internal data (Teamwork Graph)
  • Included with Premium/Enterprise plans
